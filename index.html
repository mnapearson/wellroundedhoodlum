Matthew Hendershot

Found and Chief Engineer

A 5-time Emmy nominee, Hendershot’s curiosity for personal stories and passion for collaborating with early-career artists continues to guide the creative ethos of his production company, Well Rounded Hoodlum.

By combining his technical and production dexterity with a core belief in the community experience, Hendershot is here to support artists on their next creative journeys by reimagining what the traditional production space can make possible.

Raised on the outskirts of Kansas, Hendershot eschewed the status quo of his rural upbringing and instead pursued his first degree in technical theatre before completing his Masters at the Conservatory of Recording Arts and Sciences. Inspired by his initial gigs in various night clubs, sound studios, and DIY producing, he went on to establish Well Rounded Hoodlum in Brooklyn, NY in 2010.

Under Well Rounded Hoodlum, Hendershot has utilized his multidisciplinary skill set to contract with companies like Adobe, Amazon, Apple, Ford Motor Company and MSL, along with working as an associate producer with Google. Drawing on his presenter background, Hendershot is currently the co-host and lead producer of the podcast “Brave New Weed” as well as the host and producer of his own podcast, “I’ll Take a Shot at That.”

Recognizing in Leipzig the same kind of ambitious, generative spirit that so defines neighborhoods in Austin, Brooklyn and Berlin, Hendershot wants Well Rounded Hoodlum to be an accessible, multimedia bridge for makers of all disciplines and backgrounds. Moreover, with a focus on community-building, he aspires to foster an evolving collaborative network, one that allows individuals to be in conversation with each other’s work while simultaneously drawing on individual expertise. Thus, no matter the needs or medium of your project, Hendershot is ready to make it happen alongside a collective of diverse, uniquely skilled professionals.
